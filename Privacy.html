Privacy Policy

Hey there, privacy enthusiasts! We get it—protecting your personal info is as important as not dropping your ice cream cone. So, here’s how we make sure your data stays safe and sound:

Information We Collect

So, what juicy info do we gather?

  • Your name and email address (we promise not to send you spam… unless it's the edible kind)
  • Your likes and dislikes, because we're nosey like that
  • Other stuff you share with us, like your favorite mythical creature or whether you prefer pancakes or waffles

How We Use the Information

We use your info to make your experience as magical as possible:

  • Keep a record of your favorite myths and legends
  • Improve our services so we can be the best myth and folklore website in the universe
  • Send you emails about new stories or special offers (because who doesn't love a good tale?)
  • Tailor the website to fit your mythical tastes

Security

We’ve got more security layers than an ogre has onions. Your info is as safe as a dragon in its lair. We use tech stuff (that we can barely pronounce) to keep it secure.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies! Not the chocolate chip kind (sadly). These digital cookies help our website remember who you are. They’re like little pieces of magical memory that make your browsing experience smoother than a unicorn’s mane.

Controlling Your Personal Information

Feeling a bit like Gandalf? You shall pass… your control over your info:

  • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking the ‘unsubscribe’ link faster than a pixie on caffeine
  • Send us a raven (or just an email) if you want to change your preferences

We promise not to sell your info to shady characters or trolls. Seriously, your data is yours, and we respect that.
