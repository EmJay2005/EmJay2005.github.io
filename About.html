About Us

Welcome to Philippine Mythology and Folklores! We're your one-stop shop for all things mystical, magical, and downright legendary. Our mission? To bring the enchanting world of Filipino myths and legends to life and preserve these captivating stories for future generations. Think of us as the modern-day bards, spinning tales of heroism, mystery, and wonder.

Our team of dedicated storytellers and researchers is as passionate about Philippine mythology as a Kapre is about his cigar. We dive deep into the heart of Filipino culture, unearthing tales that have been passed down through generations. From the heroic exploits of legendary figures to the whimsical adventures of mythical creatures, we’ve got it all.

So, whether you’re a long-time enthusiast or a curious newcomer, join us on this epic journey. Discover the stories that have shaped the soul of the Filipino people, and maybe even find your new favorite legend. Let’s explore this enchanting world together—no enchanted amulets required!
